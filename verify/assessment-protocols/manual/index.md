## Protocol Types

Manual verification can begin with one of five review procedures:

1. **Research-form legitimacy check:** confirm that scope, burden, artifacts, release state, and failure modes are visible.
2. **Derivation trace check:** choose a result, follow its Corpus support, and inspect available formalization links.
3. **Bridge adequacy check:** identify which transfer claim is being made and whether the bridge supports that transfer.
4. **Prediction / falsification review:** inspect formula, timing, empirical target, and stated failure condition.
5. **Corpus / Result alignment check:** verify that a public result is backed by the IDs, dependencies, and status labels it cites.
