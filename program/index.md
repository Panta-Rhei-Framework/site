## The research contract

The Program lane explains what the Panta Rhei Research Program is trying to do and what would count as failure.

The claim is deliberately ambitious: a constrained formal kernel is used to construct a research corpus with projections into mathematics, physics, life, and metaphysics. That scope is only meaningful if the program also exposes its constraints, methods, verification routes, and correction surfaces.

## What the program refuses

The program should not be read as a loose metaphor, a collection of analogies, or a conventional theory-of-everything landing page. It is a research build with explicit scope labels and public accountability surfaces.
