## What this approach tries to solve

Structural realism emphasizes the role of structure in scientific realism. Ontic structural realism gives structure and relations ontological priority over individual objects in some interpretations. See the Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy entry on [Structural Realism](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/structural-realism/).

## What Panta Rhei shares

Panta Rhei shares a structure-first orientation, skepticism toward substance-first ontology, relational primacy, and concern for how science can refer to reality through structure.

The shared instinct is that object language should not silently dictate metaphysics.

## Where Panta Rhei differs

Panta Rhei does not only assert that structure is fundamental.

It requires a kernel, construction spine, core semantics, corpus, result-status grammar, verification routes, and no-externalities discipline.

Ontic structural realism gives priority to structure. Panta Rhei asks what construction can make that priority inspectable.
