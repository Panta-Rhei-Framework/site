## About This Volume (First Edition)

Book II lifted τ to three dimensions through the τ³ fibration (τ¹ base × T² fiber), developing the lemniscate boundary, spectral character algebra, holomorphic analysis, Hartogs extension, residue theory, connections to number theory, and the Central Theorem: O(τ³) ≅ A_spec(L).

## What Changed in the Second Edition

The Second Edition refines the split-complex holomorphy framework, strengthens the mutual-determination results (5-way equivalence), deepens the self-enrichment mechanism, and integrates the full Lean 4 formalization (80% registry coverage for Book II). The subtitle changes from the longer descriptive form to "Finite Readouts of Infinity."

## Archival Note

This volume belongs to the archived First Edition of the Panta Rhei series. It remains part of the publication history of the program, but it has been superseded by the corresponding Second Edition volume as the current canonical release.
