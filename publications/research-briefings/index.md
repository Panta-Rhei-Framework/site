## What Research Briefings are

Research Briefings are framework-grounded synthesis and translation artifacts.

They translate existing Panta Rhei Results, assumptions, and verification status into a domain, institution, public-good context, or applied audience.

Research Briefings are framework-grounded synthesis and translation artifacts. They do not primarily introduce new technical research claims; they translate existing Results, assumptions, and verification status into domain-facing contexts.

## What Research Briefings are not

Research Briefings are not primary Research Papers. They do not primarily introduce new technical results. They are structured briefings for readers who need to understand conditional implications.
