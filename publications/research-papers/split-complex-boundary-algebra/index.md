## Summary

This paper develops the split-complex boundary algebra used by the Panta Rhei framework. It gives a countable profinite construction, a canonical uniqueness argument, and the four-atom generator dictionary connecting algebraic boundary structure to the hinge-paper sequence.

## Claim Boundary

The paper presents an internal structural construction and its mathematical role in the program. It does not claim independent empirical validation.
