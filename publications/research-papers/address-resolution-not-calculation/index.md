## Summary

This paper develops the address-resolution view of arithmetic in Category τ. It presents canonical normalisation, the genealogical DAG, the Cayley word metric, and the claim that arithmetic in the tau setting is implemented as finite-witness address resolution rather than equational calculation.

## Claim Boundary

The paper states a framework-internal mathematical architecture. It should not be read as an external empirical confirmation of the framework.
