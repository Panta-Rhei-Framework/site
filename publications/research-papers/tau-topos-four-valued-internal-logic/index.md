## Summary

This paper develops the tau-topos and its four-valued internal logic, using split-complex idempotents and omega-germ stabilization to address paraconsistent semantic circularity in the Panta Rhei framework.

## Claim Boundary

The paper gives a framework-internal logical architecture. Comparisons to established topoi, paraconsistent logics, and semantic theories require the explicit distinctions stated in the paper.
