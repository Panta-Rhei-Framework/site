## Choose the Right Door

Engagement should match the kind of attention you want to bring. The program is open to readers, critics, reviewers, institutions, and engineering contributors, but each role needs a different route.

<div class="v2-grid v2-grid-2">
  <a class="v2-tile" href="{{ '/discover/' | relative_url }}">
    <h3>First-time reader</h3>
    <p>Use Discover for a guided orientation before entering the denser lanes.</p>
  </a>
  <a class="v2-tile" href="{{ '/verify/how-to-audit/' | relative_url }}">
    <h3>Reviewer or critic</h3>
    <p>Use the audit routes to inspect claims by mathematical, physical, philosophical, or formal-methods stance.</p>
  </a>
  <a class="v2-tile" href="{{ '/engage/follow-the-research/' | relative_url }}">
    <h3>Follower</h3>
    <p>Receive substantive research notes and release updates when there is something real to report.</p>
  </a>
  <a class="v2-tile" href="{{ '/engage/for-engineering-contributors/' | relative_url }}">
    <h3>Engineering contributor</h3>
    <p>Contribute to TauLib, documentation hygiene, registry bookkeeping, and related verification infrastructure.</p>
  </a>
</div>
