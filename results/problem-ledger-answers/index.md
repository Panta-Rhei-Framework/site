## Exact Problem Ledger Mirror

Problem Answers are the one-to-one Results mirror of the public Problem Ledger. Each public Problem Ledger item has exactly one Problem Answer page that states the current framework answer, boundary, or backlog decision.

Generic Result pages remain live as supporting evidence, landmarks, and broader readouts. They do not replace the exact Problem Ledger mirror.

<div class="notice note"><strong>Status note.</strong> A Problem Answer reports the program's internal stance. It does not imply external verification, scientific acceptance, or consensus.</div>
