## Scope

This section concerns educational pathways into complex science, curriculum implications, guided public understanding, teaching coherence across disciplines, and democratized access to difficult foundational material.

## Core Idea

If the framework is valid and teachable, it may enable new ways of presenting mathematics, physics, life, and metaphysics as an intelligible whole. The educational impact would lie in orientation and structure: helping readers see which claims are formal, empirical, interpretive, or conditional.

## Limits

Educational usefulness does not prove the framework true. It is a consequence surface, not a verification surface.
