## Scope

This section concerns downstream scientific research design, new modeling possibilities, interdisciplinary research programs, follow-on theorem and model development, and domain-specific research agendas.

## Core Idea

The framework may matter not only as a theory of reality, but as a generator of applied research pathways. That claim remains conditional: an applied program earns relevance only when the result it depends on is visible in [Results]({{ '/results/' | relative_url }}) and inspectable in [Verify]({{ '/verify/' | relative_url }}).

## Research Shifts

- **From isolated models to translation layers**: applied work must name how a formal result enters a domain.
- **From parameter tuning to structural pressure**: the most interesting applied programs are those where fewer knobs create stronger tests.
- **From papers alone to toolchains**: TauLib, registries, release manifests, and reproducible artifacts become part of the research surface.
- **From one-domain adoption to cross-domain comparison**: domains can ask whether the same structural claim behaves differently under different uptake conditions.
