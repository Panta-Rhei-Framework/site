## LinkedIn — long-form (1-2 paragraphs)

### General announcement (long-form)

> **Announcing the Panta Rhei Research Program** — an independent open research program dedicated to building a coherent theory of reality. The public site is an inspectable research observatory: Agenda for obligations, Corpus for construction, Results for consequences, Verify for inspection, Impact for conditional relevance, and Engage for open scrutiny.
>
> What's distinctive: every claim is published with its **scope label**, every internal proof is **Lean 4-formalized**, and every prediction ships with its own **falsification test**. The program is independent research — not yet peer-reviewed in traditional journals — but the verification surfaces are open from day one.
>
> If you work in any of these domains, the [Reviewer's Dossier]({{ '/assets/media/reviewers-dossier.pdf' | relative_url }}) is the structured entry point. The full media kit (including 14 PDFs, the BibTeX bibliography, and the visual atlas) lives at {{ site.url }}{{ '/media/' | relative_url }}.

### Decisive-test framing (long-form)

> A research program that publishes a **decisive falsification test** alongside its central claim is rare. The Panta Rhei Research Program does this on day one: the framework predicts the CMB-S4 tensor-to-scalar ratio r ≈ ι_τ⁴ ≈ 0.0136. CMB-S4 (operational ~2030) will measure r to a precision that distinguishes this prediction from competing inflationary models.
>
> If r ≈ 0.0136, the prediction succeeds. If r is materially different, the framework is in serious trouble. **The framework is committed in advance.**
>
